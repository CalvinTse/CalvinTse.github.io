Projects

Automated Plant Watering System

This project pairs the Raspberry Pi with the Arduino Expansion Shield, and DFRobot sensors to detect change in moisture, temperture, and light changes in the surrounding. The information collected by the sensors are sent to Microsoft Azure's Iot Hub via Azure ConnecttheDots and then gets displayed through PowerBI. This system also waters the plant once the moisture in the soil falls below a certain value.
